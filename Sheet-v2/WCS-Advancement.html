Scholar Techniques
JobClassForesightBase: Reaction, 1/Round
Trigger: Trigger conditions vary.

Your studies have left you with uncanny prescience. When a condition in the following list is met, you may perform the action.
• When you fail a recall knowledge check and used a specialized knowledge, try again.
• When initiative is determined, add +2 to your initiative.
• When initiative is determined, gain 1 Mana.
• When you are surprised, you may choose to act in the surprise round.
• When an adjacent ally starts their turn, you allow the character to use the Dash technique once for free.

Saw That ComingAugment: Foresight
Resources: 1 Mana

Instead you have the following options:
• When a character becomes engaged with you, you may move half your speed.
• When an adjacent ally is targetted by an attack, you may use the shove technique on them before the attack occurs. If the ally is not in the attacker's range, the attack automatically misses.
• When an adjacent ally gains a condition that wasn’t caused by one of their own techniques, clear the condition.

This augment can only be used 1/Rest

As You May RecallAugment: Foresight
Resources: 1 Chakra

Instead you may declare a flashback scene. The scene cannot be more than 1 minute long and having happened no more than a day's time. The scene may show you having prepared for an outcome. This may come in the form of having purchased/acquired a necessary item or having gathered information related to the present.

This flashback must be possible in accordance to established events. The GM may declare you need to perform a skill check to have the scene occur. The GM is the final arbiter of whether a flashback can be established and what the consequences are.

This augment can only be used 1/Day

PassiveClassEclectic KnowledgeBase

Whenever a nearby character fails a recall knowledge check and you have not yet attempted to perform the same check, you may perform the same check with +1 advantage.

Point of ClarityAugment: Eclectic Knowledge

Whenever a nearby character fails a recall knowledge check and you are trained in the same skill, you may add +4 to their check by correcting any gaps in their knowledge.
