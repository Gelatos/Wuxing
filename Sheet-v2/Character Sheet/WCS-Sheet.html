Growths
As your character advances in level they will increase their ability scores based on the growth rates of their class and starting growths. Each time you level you will gain progress in each ability score (and some statistics) equal to your growths total. All characters begin with a pool of 16 points. These points can be assigned to each of the eight ability scores, to a maximum of 3 points in any stat. Each point will grant the ability score +0.2 growth.

▣ ▢ Brace
Brace represents a character's ability to resist a physical force and shrug it off by holding strong and blocking. Common uses of this defense are to prevent a fast attack from harming the character or to resist the effect of many pushing effects.
▿ ▹
▣ ▢ Fortitude
Fortitude is a character's ability to resist internal damage such as from poisons or sickness.
▿ ▹
▣ ▢ Presence
Presence is a character's ability to force their will over their environment. It is used to disrupt ether as it means to affect a character.
▿ ▹
▣ ▢ Reflex
Reflex is used when a character could quickly react to a situation with movement. It is usually used to avoid powerful attacks or to get out of the way of harmful effects that only need to touch the character like a fireball.

▿ ▹
▣ ▢ Insight
Insight represents a character's ability to parse conversation and judge mental states. This defense is typically used when information is being hidden in text or speech or to detect when someone is concealing their true thoughts.
▿ ▹
▣ ▢ Notice
Notice is the ability to see or hear sudden changes in your environment. It is typically used to counter a character's stealth attempts or to hear a distant or quiet noise.
▿ ▹
▣ ▢ Resolve
Resolve is the ability to persevere when your will is attacked. It is used to defend against intimidation and to stay motivated when desperation sets in.
