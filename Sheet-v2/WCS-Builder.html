Growths
As your character advances in level they will increase their ability scores based on the growth rates of their class and starting growths. Each time you level you will gain progress in each ability score (and some statistics) equal to your growths total. All characters begin with a pool of 12 points. These points can be assigned to each of the eight ability scores, to a maximum of 3 points in any stat. Each point will grant the ability score +0.2 growth.
