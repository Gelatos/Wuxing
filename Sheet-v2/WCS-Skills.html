▣ ▢ Finesse (QCK)
Finesse is used to strike with light and nimble weapons such as daggers, shortswords, and rapiers. ▣ ▢ Skirmish (PRC)
This skill governs most balanced, close range fighting styles such as with longswords, clubs, and polearms. Unique to this group are weapons that can exploit both reflex and brace vulnerabilities.
